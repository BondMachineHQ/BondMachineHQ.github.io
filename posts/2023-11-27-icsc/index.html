Lecture at the ICSC Course: Introductory course to HLS FPGA programming

November 27, 2023

Lecture at the ICSC Course: Introductory course to HLS FPGA programming, a course thought for the new personnel hired by means of the ICSC project to work on projects and research activities in the framework of Spoke 2 (“Fundamental Research And Space Economy”). It has been an occasion to build new documentation and hands-on material for the BondMachine project now available in the BM repository. Slides on the docs section of the site.
