Zora

Zora is the codename for the second BondMachine prototipe, the first with a monitor and a keyboard. It is based on a Basys3 evaluation board. To build it you also need a 3D printer and a tool to cut and engraving the plexi.

The result of the process will be something like this:

Shopping list

   
Digilent Basys3
7 inch VGA HDMI monitor (like the 7300101463 E231732)
An USB (power) to jack female (5.5mm - 2.1mm)
A couple (male female) of jack connector (5.5mm - 2.1mm)
An usb type A female to micro USB male (10 cm)
An usb type A female to male (10 cm)
Some spare wire
A short VGA cable (3cm) or eventually 2 male VGA connectors to solder it
A 2 pin dupont female connector
Some 3D printer silk
Some plexiglass (3mm) and a tool to cut and engraving it
